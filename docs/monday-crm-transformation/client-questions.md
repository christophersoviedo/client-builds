# Client Questions

## Business And Sales Process

- What products or services are sold through this CRM?
- Who is the target customer?
- What does a qualified lead mean for your team?
- What are the current sales stages from first inquiry to closed-won?
- What are the main reasons deals are lost?
- What handoff happens after a deal is won?
- Are renewals, upsells, or account management part of this project?

## Team And Roles

- Who will use Monday.com daily?
- Who owns lead assignment?
- Who manages sales reps and pipeline reviews?
- Who needs dashboard access?
- Who should have admin permissions?
- Are there fields or boards that should be restricted?

## Current Tools

- What tools currently collect leads?
- What email and calendar systems are used?
- Are proposals, quotes, or contracts created in a separate tool?
- What accounting, invoicing, or payment tools are used?
- Is there an existing CRM?
- Are there marketing automation or newsletter tools in place?

## Data And Migration

- Is existing CRM or spreadsheet data being migrated?
- How many contacts, companies, leads, and deals need to be imported?
- Are there known duplicate or outdated records?
- Which fields are required in the current data?
- Which historical records should be archived instead of imported?
- Who will approve the cleaned import file?

## Boards And Workflow

- Should leads and deals be separate boards?
- Should contacts and companies be managed as separate records?
- What fields are required for a lead to become a deal?
- What fields are required for a deal to be closed-won?
- What fields are required for a deal to be closed-lost?
- Do sales activities need to be tracked as tasks, board items, updates, or synced events?

## Automations

- How should new leads be assigned?
- What follow-up timing is expected for new leads?
- When should a manager be notified?
- What should happen automatically when a deal is won?
- What should happen automatically when a deal is lost?
- Should external emails be automated, or should Monday.com create reminders only?

## Dashboards

- What are the top three metrics leadership needs to see?
- Do you need reporting by rep, source, product, territory, or service type?
- Do you track deal value?
- Do you use probability-weighted forecasting?
- What counts as an overdue follow-up?
- How often will the team review dashboards?

## Integrations

- Which systems must connect to Monday.com?
- Is one-way sync enough, or is two-way sync required?
- Who has admin access to connected tools?
- Are API keys or integration permissions available?
- Are there compliance requirements around customer communication or data storage?

## Launch And Adoption

- Who will test the CRM before launch?
- Should launch happen all at once or by workflow phase?
- What training format is preferred?
- Who will maintain automations and dashboards after launch?
- What does a successful first 30 days look like?
